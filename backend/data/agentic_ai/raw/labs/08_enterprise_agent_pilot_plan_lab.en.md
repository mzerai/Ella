# Lab 8 — Build an Enterprise Agent Pilot Plan

## Mission

You must build a 60-day or 90-day pilot plan for an enterprise AI agent.

Choose one agent:

- internal support agent;
- admission agent;
- HR agent;
- finance agent;
- compliance agent;
- customer service agent;
- purchasing agent.

Your objective is to show that the agent can be tested with real users without excessive risk.

---

## Proposed situation

An organization has a prototype internal support agent.

The agent can:

- read tickets;
- search procedures;
- prepare drafts;
- create internal notes;
- escalate sensitive cases.

Management wants to deploy it across the whole organization.

You must propose a limited and governed pilot.

## Constraints

Your plan must:

- reject immediate wide deployment;
- start with limited scope;
- include observation or assistance before action;
- name owners;
- plan communication, training, and support;
- include quality, security, adoption, business, and operations KPIs;
- define stop and scaling criteria;
- include rollback or deactivation;
- prepare the transition to the final lab.

---

## What Ella will evaluate

Ella will check whether you:

- structure a realistic pilot;
- limit the scope;
- define roles;
- prepare users;
- plan support and communication;
- choose useful KPIs;
- identify risks and guardrails;
- define a clear final decision.

---

## Hints

- A pilot is not production.
- A useful agent must have an owner.
- Adoption must be prepared.
- A security KPI can block scaling.
- Pilot support reveals real problems.
